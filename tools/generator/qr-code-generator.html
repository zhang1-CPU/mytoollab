FAQ

What is a QR code?

A QR code is a two-dimensional barcode that stores data such as URLs, text, or commands scannable by smartphones.

Do the QR codes expire?

No. Static QR codes generated on this page encode data directly and never expire.

Can I print and use these QR codes?

Yes. You can download the PNG at your chosen size and embed it in flyers, business cards, or stickers.
