Merge PDFs

Combine multiple PDF files into one, in the order you choose. Arrange pages by dragging thumbnails and download the result.

📑
Drop PDFs here or click to choose files
Multiple PDFs. Combine all other content

How it works

  1. Drop one or more PDFs onto the drop zone.
  2. Reorder files by dragging thumbnails, or click Sort by name.
  3. Click Build merged PDF and download the combined file.

What is it?

A browser-only PDF merger. It reads each PDF, extracts individual page objects, composes them into a new pages tree with a new xref table. No uploads, no tracking.

Use cases

  • Combining scanned documents into a single report.
  • Stacking presentation slides and handouts together.
  • Packaging related e-sign packets for archives.
