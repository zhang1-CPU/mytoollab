How it works

Simply enter your birth date in the input above. The tool compares it with the current date on your device and computes the difference in years, months, days — plus the totals in weeks, hours, minutes, and seconds — using the local time zone on your computer.

All calculations happen in your browser. Your birth date is never sent to a server.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is this age calculator?

The calculator uses your device clock and the Gregorian calendar. It accounts for leap years and is accurate to the second.

Does it account for leap years?

Yes. Leap years and months with different day counts are handled automatically by the JavaScript Date engine.

What time zone is used?

The tool uses your device's local time zone for the "today" reference point.

Is my data stored anywhere?

No. All computations are done locally in your browser. Nothing is transmitted or stored.

What is Age Calculator?

An age calculator determines the exact age of a person or entity from a given birth date to today's date or any target date. It calculates the total years, months, days, and even hours or minutes between two dates.

How to use

  1. Step 1: Enter the birth date in the day, month, and year fields.
  2. Step 2: Optionally enter today's date or a target date.
  3. Step 3: The calculator instantly displays your exact age in years, months, and days.

Use cases

  • Calculating a person's exact age for official documents and forms.
  • Finding out how many days until a birthday, anniversary, or event.
  • Verifying age eligibility for services, subscriptions, or legal requirements.
  • Tracking the age of historical landmarks, buildings, or companies.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the age calculation accurate for leap years?

Yes. The calculator accounts for leap years and all calendar variations to provide an exact age in years, months, and days.

Can I calculate age at a future date?

Yes. Simply enter the birth date and a future target date to see how old someone will be on that specific day.

How do I calculate age in months or days only?

The result shows years, months, and days together. The total number of days is also displayed for precision.
