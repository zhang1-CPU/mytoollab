About Image Resizer

Image Resizer is a free online tool that changes image dimensions while maintaining quality. Simply upload your image, enter new width and height values in pixels, and download the resized result. The tool supports locking aspect ratio to prevent distortion and offers preset sizes for popular social media platforms.

All resizing happens locally in your browser using the Canvas API, ensuring your images never leave your device. Choose output format between JPG, PNG, and WebP depending on your needs. The tool is perfect for preparing images for web, social media, email attachments, or any application requiring specific dimensions.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I resize an image?

Upload an image, then enter new dimensions in pixels or use preset sizes. Check 'Lock aspect ratio' to maintain proportions. Click Download when done.

What are the quick presets for?

Presets include common social media sizes like Instagram Post (1080×1080), YouTube (1280×720), Twitter Header (1500×500), and more.

What does Lock aspect ratio do?

When locked, changing width automatically adjusts height (and vice versa) to keep the original proportions. This prevents distortion.

What output formats are supported?

You can save as JPG, PNG, or WebP. JPG is best for photos, PNG preserves transparency, WebP offers best compression.

Are my images uploaded to a server?

No. All resizing happens locally in your browser. Your images never leave your device.
