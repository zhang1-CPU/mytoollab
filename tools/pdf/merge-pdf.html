Combine multiple PDF files into a single document. Drag and drop, reorder pages, and download your merged file — all done privately in your browser.

How to Merge PDF Files

  1. Upload PDFs: Click "Choose Files" or drag & drop your PDF documents into the upload area.
  2. Reorder (optional): Use the up/down arrows on each file to arrange them in your preferred order.
  3. Merge: Click the "Merge & Download" button. The tool will combine pages in the listed order.
  4. Download: Your merged PDF will be downloaded automatically as merged.pdf.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Merge PDF tool really free?

Yes. Our PDF merger is 100% free to use with no signup or watermark.

Do you store my uploaded PDF files?

No. All processing happens locally in your browser using pdf-lib. Your files never leave your device.

Can I reorder pages before merging?

Yes. Uploaded files are listed with move up/down controls so you can arrange them in the correct order before merging.

How many PDFs can I merge at once?

You can merge dozens of PDF files at once. We recommend keeping total file size under 100MB for the best performance.

Which browsers are supported?

Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge and Opera on desktop and mobile devices are all supported.
