Frequently Asked Questions

What thumbnail qualities are available?

YouTube provides thumbnails in several resolutions: Max Res (1280×720 HD), SD (640×480), HQ (480×360), MQ (320×180), and Small (120×90).

Why does the thumbnail show a placeholder?

Some videos, especially newer ones or those with restricted embedding, may not have the max resolution thumbnail available. Try a lower quality option in that case.

Can I download thumbnails from any YouTube video?

Yes. As long as the YouTube video is public or unlisted, you can download its thumbnail using this tool.

Are downloaded thumbnails free to use?

Thumbnails are sourced from YouTube's public servers. You can use them for personal projects, but commercial use may be subject to YouTube's terms and the video creator's rights.

Does this work with video IDs?

Yes. You can paste either a full YouTube URL (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=...) or just the 11-character video ID (e.g., dQw4w9WgXcQ).
