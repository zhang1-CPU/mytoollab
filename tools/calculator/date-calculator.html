Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate business days between two dates?

Use the 'Business Days' mode. Select your start and end dates, choose whether to exclude weekends, and optionally add holidays in YYYY-MM-DD format separated by commas.

Are the results inclusive or exclusive?

The date difference includes the start date but not the end date by default. For example, from Jan 1 to Jan 2 would be 1 day.

Can I add or subtract years?

Yes. In 'Add/Subtract Days' mode, you can enter any positive or negative number of days. Use negative numbers to subtract days.

What date formats are supported for holidays?

Enter holidays in YYYY-MM-DD format, separated by commas. For example: 2026-01-01, 2026-12-25, 2026-08-15.
