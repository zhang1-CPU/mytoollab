How to Scan QR Codes

  1. Choose method: Decide whether to upload an image (From Image tab) or use your camera (From Camera tab).
  2. Upload or enable: Drop an image or click "Start Camera" and allow permissions.
  3. Scan: If using camera, point it at a QR code and it will auto-decode.
  4. Copy & use: The decoded text or URL will appear below. Click to copy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the QR scanner really free?

Yes. Scan as many QR codes as you like for free.

Do you store the scanned data?

No. QR decoding runs locally in your browser using jsQR.

Which camera does it use?

It uses your device's rear or front camera via the browser's getUserMedia API.

Can I scan from an image file?

Yes. You can also upload a QR code image and decode it.

What about my privacy?

Images and camera frames are processed locally — nothing is uploaded.
