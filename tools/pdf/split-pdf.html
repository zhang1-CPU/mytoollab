How to Split a PDF

  1. Upload: Click "Choose File" or drop your PDF into the upload area.
  2. Choose pages: Enter a page range like 1-3,5,7-10. Or pick "Every N pages" mode.
  3. Split: Click "Split & Download" and your file(s) will be generated.
  4. Download: One or more PDF files will be downloaded automatically.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Split PDF really free?

Yes. Our PDF splitter is 100% free to use with no signup required.

No. All processing happens locally in your browser using pdf-lib.

Can I extract a specific range of pages?

Yes. Enter ranges like "1-3" or individual pages like "5,7,9-12" in the input field.

Will the output PDFs have watermarks?

No. We never add watermarks or branding to your output files.

All modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge and Opera.
