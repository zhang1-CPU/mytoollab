How to Resize Images

  1. Upload: Drop an image or click "Choose Image" to upload a JPG, PNG, or WebP file.
  2. Set dimensions: Enter width or height in pixels, or set a percentage value.
  3. Preview: The preview updates instantly in your browser.
  4. Download: Click "Download Resized Image" to save the file.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the image resizer free?

Yes. Resize as many images as you want for free.

Do you store my images?

No. Everything happens in your browser using HTML5 Canvas.

Which formats are supported?

JPG, PNG, and WebP are supported as input. Output can be JPG, PNG, or WebP.

Can I keep the aspect ratio?

Yes. Toggle the "Lock aspect ratio" checkbox to keep proportions.

What is the maximum size?

Images up to several megabytes work well. We recommend files under 25 MB for best performance.
