What is Word Counter?

A word counter counts words, characters (with and without spaces), sentences, paragraphs, reading time (200 wpm), speaking time (130 wpm) and top keyword density in real-time as you type or paste text. It's a fundamental tool for writers, students, and anyone who needs to meet character or word limits.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does it count words in Chinese, Japanese or Korean?

Yes. Each CJK character is counted individually as one word, which matches common counter conventions for those languages. Space-separated languages (English, Spanish, etc.) continue to use whitespace boundaries.

What's the difference between Characters and Characters (no spaces)?

Characters includes every code unit including spaces, tabs and newlines. Characters (no spaces) excludes any whitespace character.

How are reading and speaking time calculated?

Reading time uses 200 words per minute, speaking time uses 130 words per minute — both industry-standard defaults.

What is keyword density?

The 10 most frequent tokens of 3 or more characters in your text, along with their counts — useful for quick content analysis and SEO checks.
