Frequently Asked Questions

Why do designers use Lorem Ipsum instead of real text?

Lorem Ipsum is used because it has a near-normal distribution of letter frequencies, making it look like real text without actually being readable. This prevents viewers from focusing on the meaning of the words rather than the design elements.

Can I generate text with specific word counts?

Yes. Set the words per sentence value to control average word count. The tool generates sentences with slight randomization around your chosen target, producing natural variation.

Is Lorem Ipsum still relevant for modern web design?

Absolutely. Even with modern CMS platforms, designers still need placeholder content for wireframes, prototypes, and pitch decks where final copy is not yet available.

What is the history of Lorem Ipsum?

Lorem Ipsum text is derived from a section of Cicero's De Finibus Bonorum et Malorum (On the Ends of Goods and Evils), a philosophical work from 45 BC. It was popularized in the 1960s with the release of Letraset sheets.

Can I use this for SEO testing?

While Lorem Ipsum is not meaningful content, you can use it to test how search engines handle pages with specific word counts, heading structures, and meta elements without the variable of real content.
