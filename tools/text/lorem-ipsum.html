Frequently Asked Questions

What is Lorem Ipsum?

Lorem Ipsum is placeholder text used in design and publishing. It looks like Latin but is actually meaningless, allowing designers to focus on layout without being distracted by readable content.

How do I customize the generated text?

Set the number of paragraphs, sentences per paragraph, and words per sentence using the input fields before clicking Generate.

Can I copy directly to my design tool?

Yes. Click Copy to copy all generated text to your clipboard, then paste it directly into your design, document, or website.

Is the generated text always different?

Yes. The tool uses randomized word selection from a Lorem Ipsum word bank, so each generation produces unique text.
