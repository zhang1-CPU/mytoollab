Frequently Asked Questions

How does AI content detection work?

The tool analyzes text patterns commonly associated with AI-generated content, including sentence structure, vocabulary variety, predictability, and the use of certain phrases. It uses heuristic-based detection rather than machine learning models.

How accurate is the detection?

Accuracy varies. The tool is designed to provide indicators rather than definitive judgments. Use it as one factor among many when evaluating content authenticity.

Why might human content be flagged as AI?

Some writing styles, especially formal or academic writing, can share characteristics with AI-generated content. This is why results should be considered as indicators, not proof.

Can I use this to check student essays?

Yes, but with caution. The tool can provide insights, but shouldn't be used as the sole determination of whether content was AI-generated.

Is my text saved?

No. All analysis happens locally in your browser. Your text is never transmitted or stored anywhere.
