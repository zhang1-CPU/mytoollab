What is Lorem Ipsum?

Lorem Ipsum is scrambled Latin-like placeholder text that has been the industry standard for mockups since the 1500s. Designers and developers use it to demonstrate visual layouts without relying on final copy, drawing focus to typography, spacing, and composition.

Use cases

  • Filling in text placeholders during web and app design.
  • Creating wireframes and prototypes for client presentations.
  • Testing typography, layout, and font rendering.
  • Producing realistic-looking mockups for pitching and proposals.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Lorem Ipsum actual Latin?

It is derived from a 1st-century BC Roman philosopher's text (De Finibus Bonorum et Malorum), scrambled and truncated. It looks like Latin but has no grammatical meaning.

Why does it start with "Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet"?

This specific line is the most recognizable opening of Lorem Ipsum. Many designers use it so that readers intuitively understand the text is placeholder.

Is the generation random?

Yes. Paragraphs, sentences, and words are pseudo-randomly selected each time you click Generate, so you can try multiple times to get a variety of lengths.
