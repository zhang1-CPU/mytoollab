How it works

  1. Your browser queries public IP services (ipify.org) to fetch your current IPv4 and IPv6 addresses.
  2. Location and ISP data is fetched from ipapi.co using your detected IP.
  3. The remaining info is extracted directly from your browser using standard JavaScript APIs.
  4. Nothing is stored — all requests are one-time and browser-based.
