Frequently Asked Questions

What is a URL slug?

A URL slug is the part of a URL that identifies a specific page in a human-readable format. For example, in /blog/how-to-create-url-slugs, the slug is 'how-to-create-url-slugs'.

What does 'SEO-friendly' mean?

An SEO-friendly slug is short, descriptive, contains keywords, uses hyphens to separate words, and avoids special characters or numbers when possible.

What are stop words?

Stop words are common words like 'a', 'the', 'in', 'on' that search engines often ignore. Removing them makes slugs shorter and more keyword-focused.

Should I use lowercase in URLs?

Yes. URLs are case-sensitive. Always use lowercase to avoid broken links. 'my-page' and 'My-Page' are treated as different URLs.

What is URL encoding?

URL encoding converts special characters into a format that can be transmitted over the internet, replacing them with % followed by hexadecimal digits (like %20 for space).

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes a URL slug SEO-friendly?

An SEO-friendly slug is short, descriptive, contains the target keyword, uses hyphens to separate words (not underscores), avoids special characters and numbers when possible, and uses all lowercase letters.

Should I include keywords in my URL slug?

Yes. Including relevant keywords in your URL helps search engines understand the page content and can improve rankings for those terms. Keep slugs concise though — 3-5 words is usually ideal.

What happens to special characters?

Special characters are removed or converted to their ASCII equivalents. For example, spaces become hyphens, and symbols like #, $, and ! are stripped entirely.

Why should I remove stop words from slugs?

Stop words like "a", "the", "in", and "on" add length without meaning. Removing them makes your URL shorter and more keyword-dense, which is generally better for SEO.

Can I use underscores instead of hyphens?

No. Search engines treat hyphens as word separators but typically ignore underscores. Always use hyphens (-) in your URL slugs for best SEO results.
