How it works

  1. Choose method: Upload an image (From Image tab) or use your camera (From Camera tab).
  2. Upload or enable: Drop an image or click "Start Camera" and allow permissions.
  3. Scan: If using camera, point at a QR code and it will auto-decode.
  4. Copy & use: The decoded text or URL will appear below.
