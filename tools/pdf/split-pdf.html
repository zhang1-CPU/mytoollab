Split PDF

Upload a PDF and split it. Choose either "one file per page" or a range expression like "1,3-5,7" to extract specific pages. All files are bundled into a ZIP for download.

📑
Drop a PDF here or click to choose a file

How it works

  1. Drop your PDF onto the drop zone or click to select.
  2. Choose either "Each page" or specify a range expression like "1,3-5,7".
  3. Click Split PDF and download the resulting ZIP.

What is it?

A browser-only PDF splitter. The original PDF is parsed, selected pages are collected, and for each requested page a new minimal PDF (1 page each) is produced and packaged into a single ZIP. Pages that cannot be parsed are skipped with a warning.

Use cases

  • Extracting invoice or receipt pages from a large batch.
  • Splitting presentations or reports before sharing specific sections.
  • Preparing e-sign envelopes per-page from one file.
