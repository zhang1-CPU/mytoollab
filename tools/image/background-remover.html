Frequently Asked Questions

Is this background remover free?

Yes. Remove backgrounds from images for free, without signup.

Do you upload my images?

No. All processing runs in your browser using HTML5 Canvas and color-difference math.

How does it detect the background?

It samples border pixels and a reference color, then removes pixels close to those colors within the chosen tolerance.

Which formats are supported?

JPG, PNG, and WebP are supported. Output is always PNG to preserve transparency.

Can I adjust the tolerance?

Yes. Move the tolerance slider until the background is cleanly removed.
