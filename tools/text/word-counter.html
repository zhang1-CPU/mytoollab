FAQ

What's the difference between "Characters" and "Trimmed"?

Characters is the raw total — every letter, space, and punctuation mark. Trimmed strips leading and trailing whitespace first, so it reflects the actual content length most APIs and platforms care about.

Is my text stored or sent anywhere?

No. Everything runs in your browser. Nothing is transmitted to any server. You can even use this tool offline once the page has loaded.

How are words counted?

Words are split on whitespace. Consecutive spaces count as one separator, so extra blank lines won't inflate your word count. Hyphenated words like "well-known" count as one word.

Does it work on mobile?

Yes. The layout, text area, and stat cards are all sized with relative units so they scale cleanly on any screen — phone, tablet, or desktop.

Is there a character or word limit?

No hard limit. The counter handles long documents, code files, and large pastes without slowing down.
