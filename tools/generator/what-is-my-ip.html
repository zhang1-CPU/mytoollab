How it works

  1. Your browser tries multiple public IP services in sequence (api.ipify.org, checkip.amazonaws.com, icanhazip.com) to fetch your IPv4 address.
  2. After an IP is found, ipapi.co is queried for location and ISP details.
  3. Browser, screen, and timezone info is read directly from your browser.
  4. No data is stored on any server. All detection is 100% client-side.
