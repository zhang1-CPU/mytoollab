How it works

  1. Your browser queries a public IP service (ipify.org) to fetch your current IPv4 and IPv6 addresses.
  2. The remaining info is extracted directly from your browser using standard JavaScript APIs (no plugins, no server-side tracking).
  3. Nothing leaves your browser except the two HTTPS calls needed to fetch your public IPs.
