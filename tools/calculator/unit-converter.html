Unit Converter

Convert between common units of length, weight, temperature, volume, area, speed, and time. All conversions are computed instantly in your browser.

How it works

  1. Pick a category and enter a value. Use the dropdowns to choose the source and target units.
  2. Linear conversions are based on a reference unit (e.g. meters for length); temperature uses the standard C/F/K formulas.
  3. Edit either input to compute in both directions, or press Swap to flip the source and target units.
