Frequently Asked Questions

What image formats can I crop?

You can crop JPG, PNG, WebP, GIF images up to 20MB in size. The tool supports most common image formats used on the web.

How do I resize without cropping?

Simply set your desired output dimensions in the 'Output Size' fields. If 'Lock aspect ratio' is checked, the other dimension will auto-adjust.

What aspect ratios are available?

Available ratios include Free, 1:1 (Instagram), 4:5 (Instagram Portrait), 9:16 (Stories), 16:9 (YouTube), 1.91:1 (Twitter), and common print sizes like A4 and Passport.

How do I save at a specific file size?

Enter your target size in KB under 'Max File Size'. The tool will adjust quality to try to meet your target. Note: PNG is lossless and won't compress significantly.

Can I batch download multiple sizes?

Yes. Click 'Download Common Sizes' to get your image in several popular sizes at once (original, thumbnail, and avatar).
