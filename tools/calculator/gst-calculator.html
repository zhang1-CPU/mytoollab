Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between Add GST and Remove GST?

Add GST (Exclusive) adds tax to a base amount. Remove GST (Inclusive) extracts the tax component from a price that already includes GST.

What are the standard GST rates in India?

Standard GST rates in India are: 0% (exempt items), 5% (essential items), 12% (standard goods), 18% (most items), and 28% (luxury items).

How is GST calculated?

For adding GST: Total = Base Amount + (Base Amount × GST Rate %). For removing GST: Base Amount = Total / (1 + GST Rate %). The GST amount is the difference.

Can I use custom GST rates?

Yes. While preset buttons are available for 5%, 12%, 18%, and 28%, you can enter any custom rate in the GST Rate input field.
