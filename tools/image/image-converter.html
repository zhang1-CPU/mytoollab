FAQ

What is the difference between formats?

JPG is great for photos with small file size. PNG supports transparency. WebP offers the best compression-to-quality ratio.

Does conversion lose quality?

Lossy formats (JPG, WebP at less than 100%) can lose quality. PNG conversion preserves quality.

Are my images uploaded?

No. Conversion happens entirely in your browser. Nothing leaves your device.
