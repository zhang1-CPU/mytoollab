How it works

Pick a category and enter a value. Changing either input recalculates the other. Linear categories (Length, Weight, Volume, Area) are based on a reference unit and a constant factor per unit. Temperature uses the standard °C / °F / K formulas.

  1. Step 1: Select the measurement category.
  2. Step 2: Choose the source unit and enter a value.
  3. Step 3: Choose the target unit; the converted value updates automatically.

Frequently Asked Questions

How precise are the conversions?

Conversions use standard factors (e.g., 1 inch = 2.54 cm exactly, 1 pound = 0.45359237 kg). Results are rounded to up to 10 significant digits.

Can I convert between metric and imperial?

Yes. Each category mixes metric and imperial units (and US customary for volume).

Why does Temperature not use the reference table like other categories?

Temperature scales are offset (not just a ratio), so the conversion formula differs for each pair.
