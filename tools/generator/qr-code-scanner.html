How it works

  1. Switch between From image (upload a picture) or From camera (live scan).
  2. Drop images onto the drop zone or click Start camera to enable the live scanner.
  3. See decoded text, copy to clipboard, and download.

What is it?

A browser-side QR code scanner. All image processing happens locally using the jsQR library; no data is uploaded and no account is required.

Use cases

  • Decoding QR codes captured from photos or screenshots.
  • Live scanning in presentations or onboarding flows.
  • Reading QR codes embedded in PDFs (via saved screenshots).

FAQs

Why does the camera not start?

Camera access must be granted by the browser. Also, camera APIs require HTTPS or localhost, and the browser must be modern (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge).

Can I scan non-QR barcodes?

This tool only decodes QR codes. For other barcode types, a different library would be needed.
