Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use keyboard with this calculator?

Yes. Use keys 0-9 for numbers, + - * / for operations, . for decimal, Enter for equals, Backspace to delete, and Escape to clear.

How does the percentage button work?

The % button calculates percentage of the current number. For example, to find 20% of 50, press 50 then % to get 0.5 (which is 1%), or use it after an operation like 50 + 10 % to add 5 (10% of 50) to 50.

What is the maximum number size?

The calculator handles numbers up to JavaScript's safe integer limit (2^53 - 1). Very large results may display in scientific notation.

Is this calculator free to use?

Yes, the Basic Calculator is completely free and all calculations are performed locally in your browser.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use this calculator on my phone?

Yes. Our basic calculator is fully responsive and works on all mobile devices. The large buttons are designed for touch input, and the interface adapts to smaller screens automatically.

How do I calculate percentages?

Enter your base number, press the operation you want (like +), enter the percentage number, then press %. For example, to add 15% to 200: 200 + 15 % = 230.

What is the maximum number this calculator can handle?

The calculator handles numbers up to JavaScript's safe integer limit (9,007,199,254,740,991 or roughly 9 quadrillion). Very large results may display in scientific notation.

Is there a keyboard shortcut for equals?

Yes. Press Enter or = on your keyboard to calculate the result. Escape clears the calculator, and Backspace deletes the last digit.

Are my calculations stored anywhere?

No. All calculations happen locally in your browser. Nothing is transmitted to any server, and no history is stored when you close the page.
