Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Privacy Policy?

A Privacy Policy is a legal document that explains how a website or app collects, uses, stores, and protects user data. It's required by law in many jurisdictions, especially for websites that collect personal information.

Do I really need a Privacy Policy?

If you collect any personal data (name, email, IP address, cookies) from users, you likely need a Privacy Policy. Laws like GDPR (EU), CCPA (California), and others require it.

Is the generated policy legally binding?

This tool generates a template based on common practices. While it can serve as a starting point, we recommend consulting a legal professional for specific legal advice.

What information do I need to generate a policy?

You'll need your website/app name, contact email, the types of data you collect (name, email, cookies, etc.), and how you use third-party services like Google Analytics or advertising.

Can I customize the generated policy?

Yes. The generator creates a template that you can edit and customize based on your specific data practices and legal requirements.
