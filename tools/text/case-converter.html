Frequently Asked Questions

What case options are available?

Options include: Sentence case (first letter capital), lowercase, UPPERCASE, Title Case (capitalize each word), Capitalized Case (every word capitalized), and Alternating Case (aLtErNaTiNg).

Does it work with special characters and numbers?

Yes. The tool preserves numbers, punctuation, and special characters while only converting letters.

Can I undo a case conversion?

Unfortunately, once converted, you cannot revert to the original. We recommend copying your original text before converting.

How does Title Case handle small words?

This tool capitalizes all words regardless of length, including articles (a, an, the), prepositions, and conjunctions. Some style guides differ, but this is the standard behavior.
