What is JPG to PDF?

A JPG to PDF converter turns one or more image files into a single PDF document. It's perfect for combining scanned pages, photos, or design files into a single shareable document.

How to use

  1. Step 1: Upload one or more JPG/PNG images.
  2. Step 2: Arrange the order by dragging images.
  3. Step 3: Set page orientation and margins.
  4. Step 4: Click 'Create PDF' to generate and download.

Use cases

  • Combining scanned documents into one PDF file.
  • Creating a PDF portfolio from design images.
  • Converting photo albums to PDF for sharing.
  • Preparing documents for digital signatures.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I convert multiple images to one PDF?

Yes. Upload all your images and they will be combined into a single multi-page PDF.

What image formats are supported?

JPG/JPEG, PNG, WebP, GIF, BMP, and TIFF formats are all supported.

Is the conversion lossless?

The PDF generation itself is lossless. JPEG images embedded in PDFs retain their compression.
