What is Remove Duplicate Lines?

A remove duplicates tool eliminates duplicate lines, words, or numbers from text. It instantly cleans lists, removes repeated entries, and sorts results — saving hours of manual editing.

How to use

  1. Step 1: Paste or type text with duplicates.
  2. Step 2: Choose whether to remove duplicate lines, words, or numbers.
  3. Step 3: Click 'Remove Duplicates' to process.
  4. Step 4: Copy the clean, unique results.

Use cases

  • Cleaning email lists and contact databases.
  • Removing duplicate entries from CSV exports and spreadsheets.
  • Deduplicating keyword lists for SEO campaigns.
  • Cleaning scraped data for analysis and reporting.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does it preserve the original order?

Yes. The first occurrence of each item is kept in its original position. Duplicate subsequent occurrences are removed.

Is the comparison case-sensitive?

Yes, by default. You can choose to ignore case when comparing items.
