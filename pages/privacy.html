Privacy Policy

We keep things simple and honest. Here's exactly how My Tool Lab handles your data.

Last updated: May 2025

🖥️ Your Browser Does the Work

Most tools on My Tool Lab run entirely inside your browser. That means your text, files, and inputs are processed locally on your device — they never leave it. No data is sent to our servers for these tools to function.

A few tools may need to contact a server (for example, to fetch external data or perform a heavy calculation). When that's the case, we'll say so on the tool page.

📝 We Don't Store What You Type

We do not intentionally log, save, or store any text or content you enter into our tools. Once you close or refresh the page, that data is gone.

We say "intentionally" because standard web infrastructure (servers, CDNs, proxies) may briefly handle request data in transit — but we don't collect or retain it.

⚠️ Heads up: If you're unsure about a tool's privacy, please don't paste passwords, personal IDs, financial details, or other sensitive information into it. When in doubt, leave it out.

📊 Analytics

We use or may use basic analytics (such as Google Analytics or a privacy-friendly alternative) to understand how people use the site — things like which tools are popular, how long pages take to load, and where visitors come from in general terms.

This helps us improve the site. It does not identify you personally.

💰 Advertising

My Tool Lab is free to use. To keep it running, we may display ads — now or in the future — through services like Google AdSense. These ad networks may use cookies to show you relevant ads based on your browsing history across other sites.

We don't control what those networks collect. You can opt out of personalized ads through your browser settings or tools like aboutads.info .

🍪 Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored in your browser. We may use them for:

  • Remembering your preferences (like dark mode or last-used settings)
  • Analytics — counting visits and understanding usage patterns
  • Advertising — served by third-party ad networks (see above)

You can block or delete cookies at any time through your browser settings. Some features may not work perfectly without them, but the core tools will.

🔗 Third-Party Services

My Tool Lab may load resources from third-party services, including:

  • Google Fonts — for typography (Google may log your IP)
  • Analytics providers — to measure site usage
  • Ad networks — to display relevant advertisements

Each of these services has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them if you have concerns. We only use services we consider reputable, but we can't control their data practices.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

If we make meaningful changes to this policy, we'll update the date at the top of this page. We won't send you an email about it, so feel free to check back occasionally if this matters to you.

✉️ Questions?

If you have any questions about this policy or how a specific tool handles data, reach out at hello@mytoollab.com. We're happy to explain anything in plain English.
