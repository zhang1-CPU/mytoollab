Frequently Asked Questions

Does the counter update in real time?

Yes. Stats update automatically as you type or paste text into the text area. No need to click any button.

How are words counted?

Words are counted by splitting text on whitespace and common punctuation. Multiple spaces don't create extra words, and punctuation attached to words is handled correctly.

What determines reading time?

Reading time assumes an average reading speed of 200 words per minute, which is typical for adults reading non-technical content.

Does it count characters with or without spaces?

Both counts are shown. 'Characters (with spaces)' includes all characters including spaces. 'Characters (no spaces)' excludes spaces and is useful for social media character limits.

How are paragraphs counted?

Paragraphs are separated by blank lines (double newlines). A single line break doesn't create a new paragraph.

About Word Counter

Our free word counter is a simple yet essential tool for anyone who writes online. Whether you are crafting tweets with character limits, writing blog posts with minimum word counts, preparing academic papers, or composing professional emails, knowing your exact word and character count helps you meet requirements and communicate more effectively.

The tool updates in real time as you type or paste text, showing words, characters with and without spaces, sentences, paragraphs, and estimated reading time. No more manually counting or worrying about exceeding social media limits.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the character limit for Twitter?

Twitter allows 280 characters per tweet. Use the 'Characters (no spaces)' count to maximize your message length while staying within limits.

How accurate is the word count?

The word counter accurately counts words by splitting on whitespace and common punctuation. Multiple spaces, leading/trailing spaces, and punctuation attached to words are handled correctly.

Can I use this for SEO content requirements?

Yes. Many SEO tools and content management systems have minimum word counts. Use the word counter to ensure your content meets these requirements before publishing.

Does the tool work on mobile devices?

Yes. The word counter works on any device with a web browser, including smartphones and tablets.

Is my text stored anywhere?

No. All text processing happens locally in your browser. Your text is never transmitted to any server and is cleared when you close or refresh the page.
