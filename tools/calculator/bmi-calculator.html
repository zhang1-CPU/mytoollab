BMI Calculator

Compute your Body Mass Index instantly using metric (kg/cm) or imperial (lb/in) units and see which weight category you fall into.

Category

WHO reference ranges: Underweight < 18.5 · Normal 18.5–25 · Overweight 25–30 · Obese > 30.

How it works

  1. Choose the unit system (Metric or Imperial) and enter your weight and height.
  2. BMI = weight(kg) / height(m)² — or 703 × weight(lb) / height(in)² for imperial.
  3. Results update live as you type; your category is color-coded for quick reference.
