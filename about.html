About My Tool Lab

My Tool Lab is a simple online tools website built to help people get quick results without signing up or installing anything. The site focuses on lightweight browser-based tools for text tasks and developer tasks, so you can use them instantly on desktop or mobile.

Our goal is to make everyday tasks faster and easier. Whether you want to count words, convert text case, format JSON, or encode Base64, the tools are designed to be clear, fast, and practical.

We keep the site minimal on purpose. That means fewer distractions, faster loading, and a better experience for people who just want to get something done.

If you find a bug or want a new tool added, please use the Contact page. We review feedback and keep improving the site over time.
