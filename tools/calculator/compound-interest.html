Frequently Asked Questions

What is compound interest?

Compound interest is interest calculated on both the initial principal and the accumulated interest from previous periods. It's often called 'interest on interest' and can significantly grow your investments over time.

What compounding frequencies are available?

You can choose from: Annually (1x/year), Semi-annually (2x/year), Quarterly (4x/year), Monthly (12x/year), or Daily (365x/year). More frequent compounding results in slightly higher returns.

What is the difference between Lump Sum and SIP modes?

Lump Sum mode calculates returns on a one-time investment. SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) mode includes regular monthly contributions in addition to your initial investment.

Is the displayed chart accurate?

Yes. The chart shows a comparison between your total invested amount (principal + contributions) and the final portfolio value including earned interest.
