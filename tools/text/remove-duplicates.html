Frequently Asked Questions

How does this tool find duplicates?

The tool compares each line with others to find exact matches. Enable 'Ignore case' to treat 'Apple' and 'APPLE' as duplicates, or 'Trim spaces' to ignore leading/trailing whitespace.

What does 'Keep First' vs 'Keep Last' mean?

'Keep First' keeps the first occurrence and removes subsequent duplicates. 'Keep Last' keeps the last occurrence and removes earlier ones.

Can I remove blank lines?

Yes. Enable 'Remove blank lines' to automatically remove empty lines from your text.

Does this work with sorted lists?

Yes. This tool removes exact duplicate lines regardless of order. If you want sorted unique lines, copy the output to our Case Converter or another text tool.

How does case sensitivity work?

When 'Ignore case' is enabled, all lines are compared in lowercase. So 'Hello', 'HELLO', and 'hello' would be treated as duplicates.

About Remove Duplicate Lines

Our free duplicate line remover helps you clean up lists, text files, and datasets by eliminating repeated entries quickly and accurately. Whether you are deduplicating email lists, cleaning up survey responses, removing duplicate entries from spreadsheets, or preparing data for import, this tool handles the job with flexible options for case sensitivity, whitespace handling, and blank line removal.

The tool processes everything locally in your browser, meaning your data never leaves your device. You can choose whether to keep the first or last occurrence of each duplicate, ignore leading and trailing whitespace, treat lines with different capitalization as equivalent, and automatically strip blank lines from your cleaned output.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between this and a unique list generator?

This tool removes exact duplicate lines while preserving the original order of your list. A unique list generator may also sort entries alphabetically or apply other transformations.

Can I use this for CSV data?

Yes. If your CSV data has one entry per line, you can use this tool to remove duplicate rows. For more complex CSV deduplication with column awareness, consider our dedicated CSV tools.

Does this tool preserve the original line order?

Yes. The tool maintains the relative order of lines in your output, only removing duplicate entries while keeping unique lines in their original sequence.

Why might I want to keep the last occurrence instead of the first?

In time-series data or version-controlled lists, the most recent entry is often the most relevant. Keeping the last occurrence ensures you retain the newest information.

Can I process very large lists?

Yes. The browser-based processing handles lists with thousands of lines efficiently. Very large lists may take a moment to process but work entirely locally without server timeouts.
