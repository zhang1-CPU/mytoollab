Frequently Asked Questions

What image formats are supported?

This tool supports JPG (JPEG), PNG, WebP, BMP, and GIF images. You can mix different formats in a single conversion.

Can I convert multiple images at once?

Yes. Simply select or drag multiple images and they will all be added to the PDF. Use the up/down arrows to reorder them.

How do I change page orientation?

Choose between Portrait or Landscape orientation from the dropdown menu. You can also select 'Fit to image' to automatically size each page to match the image dimensions.

Yes. Images are automatically centered on each page. You can add margins to add white space around the image.

Are my images uploaded to a server?

No. All processing is done locally in your browser. Your images never leave your device.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the maximum number of images I can convert at once?

There is no strict limit, but very large batches may consume significant browser memory. For best performance, we recommend converting fewer than 100 images at a time.

Can I change the order of images after adding them?

Yes. Use the up and down arrow buttons on each image row to reorder them. The final PDF will reflect this order.

Does the converter preserve image quality?

Yes. Images are embedded in the PDF at their original resolution. No compression is applied unless you specifically resize them before conversion.

Can I add the same image multiple times?

Yes. Simply add the same file multiple times to include it on multiple pages in the PDF.

What happens to images with transparency (PNG, GIF)?

Transparency is preserved in the PDF output. White or a checkerboard pattern will be used as the background where transparency exists.
