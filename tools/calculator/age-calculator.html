How it works

Simply enter your birth date in the input above. The tool compares it with the current date on your device and computes the difference in years, months, days — plus the totals in weeks, hours, minutes, and seconds — using the local time zone on your computer.

About Age Calculator

Our free age calculator is the most accurate way to determine someone's exact age from their birth date. Whether you need to calculate your own age for official documents, find out how old you will be on a specific future date, or determine age differences between people, our tool delivers precise results instantly. The calculator displays your age in years, months, weeks, days, hours, minutes, and even seconds, giving you a complete picture of how much time has passed since birth.

Unlike other age calculators that only show approximate years, our tool accounts for leap years, varying month lengths, and exact day counts to provide truly accurate calculations. This makes it perfect for legal documentation, school enrollment verification, retirement planning, and simply satisfying curiosity. The date calculator works bidirectionally — you can also find out what date you will be a certain number of days, months, or years from now.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is this age calculator?

Our age calculator is accurate to the second. It uses your device's clock and properly accounts for leap years, daylight saving time adjustments, and varying month lengths to provide precise results.

Can I calculate my age on a specific date?

Yes. Simply enter your birth date and the calculator will show your exact age at the current moment. For future dates, you can manually add time to understand your age at any point.

Does the calculator handle leap years?

Yes. The JavaScript Date engine automatically handles leap years. For example, someone born on February 29 will see their age increment on March 1 in non-leap years.

Can I calculate age difference between two people?

Yes. Enter one birth date, note the age in years, then enter the second birth date to compare. You can also calculate the exact number of days between any two dates.

Is my birth date stored or transmitted?

No. All calculations are performed locally in your browser using JavaScript. Your birth date is never sent to any server, stored, or logged anywhere.
