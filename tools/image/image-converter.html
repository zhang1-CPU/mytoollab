Image Converter

Convert images to JPG, PNG, WebP, GIF, or BMP. Upload one or many files, adjust quality and scale, and download each result or get everything in a single ZIP. Everything runs in your browser — nothing is uploaded to a server.

🖼️
Drop images here or click to choose files
Supported: JPG, PNG, WebP, GIF, BMP, SVG, HEIC (browser-dependent). Multiple files allowed.

How it works

  1. Drop images onto the drop zone or click to select files from your device.
  2. Choose an output format, adjust quality (JPG/WebP), and optionally scale or fill transparency.
  3. Download each result individually, or click Download ZIP to collect everything in one file.

What is it?

A browser-side image converter that uses the HTML5 Canvas and native image decoders. No uploads, no tracking, no account required.

Use cases

  • Converting photos to WebP or JPG for smaller file sizes on websites.
  • Preparing PNG screenshots with transparent backgrounds.
  • Rescaling or downsizing many images in a single batch.
  • Converting GIFs/BMPs to JPG or PNG for compatibility.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which formats are supported?

Output: JPG, PNG, WebP, GIF, BMP. Input: any format natively decodable by your browser (usually JPG, PNG, WebP, GIF, BMP, SVG; HEIC on modern Safari).

Will my files be uploaded?

No. Decoding and encoding happen entirely in your browser using the Canvas API. Nothing leaves your device.

What does the quality slider do?

It controls the compression quality for JPG and WebP (1–100). PNG is lossless and ignores this slider; GIF and BMP are written as-is.
