Paste your HTML and get clean, indented output instantly — right in your browser.

How to Format HTML

  1. Paste HTML: Copy your HTML markup into the "Input HTML" box.
  2. Format: Click "Format HTML" — the tool will indent, line-break, and restructure your code.
  3. Copy: Click "Copy" to place the formatted code on your clipboard.
  4. Paste anywhere: Use the beautified code in your editor or document.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this HTML formatter free?

Yes. Paste or type your HTML and beautify it for free.

How does the formatter work?

It tokenizes tags and text, then adds proper indentation and line breaks.

Does it support self-closing tags?

Yes. Self-closing tags like <br>, <img>, <input>, <hr>, <meta>, <link> are handled properly.

Can I copy the result?

Yes. Click the "Copy" button to copy the formatted HTML to your clipboard.

Do you store the HTML I enter?

No. Formatting happens in your browser and the data never leaves your device.
