What is Split PDF?

A PDF splitter extracts specific pages or page ranges from a PDF document. You can split a PDF into individual pages, extract a range, or remove unwanted pages.

How to use

  1. Step 1: Upload a PDF document.
  2. Step 2: Select pages to extract by range (e.g., 1-5, 10, 15-20).
  3. Step 3: Preview the selected pages.
  4. Step 4: Download the extracted pages as a new PDF.

Use cases

  • Extracting a chapter or section from a large PDF.
  • Splitting a scanned document into individual pages.
  • Removing unwanted pages from documents.
  • Extracting specific forms or pages from a multi-page form PDF.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I extract non-contiguous pages?

Yes. Enter page numbers separated by commas, or use ranges like '1-3, 5, 7-10'.

Is the original PDF modified?

No. The original file remains unchanged. A new PDF with only the selected pages is created.
