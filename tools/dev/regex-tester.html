Frequently Asked Questions

What regex flags are supported?

Supported flags include: g (global - find all matches), i (case insensitive), m (multiline), s (dotall - dot matches newline), and u (unicode).

How do I test a phone number regex?

Click the 'Phone' preset button to load a phone number pattern. You can customize it further by editing the regex in the pattern field.

What does 'Global' mean?

With the g flag, the regex finds all matches. Without it, only the first match is found.

Why is my regex not matching?

Check for escaping issues. In regex, special characters like . * ? need to be escaped with \ if you want to match them literally.

Can I see capture groups?

Yes. Each match shows the full matched text, position, length, and number of capture groups. Groups are shown in the position info.
