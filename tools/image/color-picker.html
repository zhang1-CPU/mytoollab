FAQ

What does HEX mean?

HEX is a hexadecimal notation for RGB colors (#RRGGBB). Each pair represents red, green, and blue values from 00 to FF.

What is the difference between RGB and RGBA?

RGBA is RGB plus an alpha (opacity) channel. An alpha of 1 means fully opaque; 0 means fully transparent.

What are HSL and HSLA?

HSL describes a color by Hue (0-360°), Saturation (0-100%), and Lightness (0-100%). HSLA adds an alpha channel.
